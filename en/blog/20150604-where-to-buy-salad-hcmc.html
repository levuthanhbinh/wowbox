Where to Buy Salad in HCMC

  • by WowBox

Bringing a new dining trend into Vietnam by focusing on a wide variety of healthy salads as the main dish, WowBox Salad Bar offers fresh and joyful experiences that you could not resist. If you are a fan of healthy diet, low-carb menu or just want to keep fit, WowBox is the perfect choice. From authentic Western flavor in Classic to fusion Signature salads, you can always find your favorite one.

To find where to buy salad in HCMC is so simple now. Located right at 70 Ham Nghi boulevard, Ben Nghe ward, District 1 - the heart of the CBD of HCMC, WowBox is within walking distance to Bitexco Financial Tower, Sunwah Tower, Continental Tower, Ben Thanh market and many other office buildings. At WowBox Salad Bar, you can easily grab a healthy salad box in no time with so many choices available: Caesar, Greek, Cobb, Italian, Nicoise, or customized peppered Thai beef, sesame tuna, smoked salmon, paprika prawn salads. You could also create your own salad with your personal favorite recipe in less than 5 minutes. Alternatively, order online at wowbox.vn or give us a call directly at 08.66859055, WowBox will ensure your chosen salads are freshly delivered to your door in the shortest time.

At WowBox Salad Bar, the quality of all vegetables and ingredients is always the top-notch concern. All fresh vegetables are sourced locally from trustworthy suppliers, abiding to very strict agricultural authority standards. WowBox uses the same source of vegetables as many famous 5-star hotels in HCMC. Immediately after supplier shipment, vegetables are manually processed with minimal steps at optimal temperatures to preserve freshness and nutrients contained. Other spices and ingredients are mostly imported to ensure their original flavors and quality in dressings and sauces. WowBox is deeply committed to providing tasty and healthy salads to our valued customers with the highest possible quality at reasonable prices.

With many choices of lettuce, over 20 standard toppings, and 15 premium toppings, WowBox Salad Bar offers a wide range of combinations that can satisfy any customers. In addition, WowBox’s menu also offers special kale salad and kale green smoothie - the number one power green in the US, a rare find in Vietnam. On top of the main ingredients of a salad like lettuces, cucumber, onion, egg, cherry tomato, carrot, croutons, tuna, salmon, beef, dressings bring in the soul for the dish. Customers can choose from authentic classic dressings such as French, Ranch, Caesar, to WowBox’s own recipe special dressings like Coriander Lime, Raspberry Balsamic, Poppy Seed.

Versatile, tasty, healthy and fresh salads, together with friendly and professional service, are WowBox Salad’s commitments to our valued customers.
