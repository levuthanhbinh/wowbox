Menu Layouts

Berg Resturant Theme offers 3 types of food menu layout, additionally these layouts can be further customized with Intro Section, you can choose to have Full Screen Intro, Half Into or No Intro at all. The Intro Section itself can take title and description. You can as well choose to have just title or description, everything or nothing at all. When you use Visual Composer you can make it even more unique and truly  yours.

You can create unlimited number of menus with different layouts and settings. You can have separate menus listing just one category or you can have menu with filters. Also you can have your menus with filters and without mixed and used together as a part of one parent element.

Berg offers you a visually appealing Mega Menu for your menu when used on your homepgae (Home tempalte).
